Harry Potter Spells

Common Spells

  • Alohomora – Unlocks locks.
  • Lumos – Creates light at the tip of the wand.
  • Nox – Extinguishes the wand light (opposite of Lumos).
  • Wingardium Leviosa – Makes objects levitate.
  • Accio – Summons objects towards the wizard.
  • Expelliarmus – Disarms the opponent.
  • Protego – Creates a protective shield.
  • Reparo – Repairs broken objects.
  • Silencio – Silences a person or creature.
  • Aparecium – Reveals invisible ink.
  • Sonorus – Amplifies the user's voice.
  • Quietus – Restores the voice to normal volume (opposite of Sonorus).

Defense and Attack Spells

  • Stupefy – Stuns the opponent.
  • Petrificus Totalus – Completely paralyzes the target.
  • Impedimenta – Stops or slows down the opponent.
  • Rictusempra – Causes intense tickling.
  • Reducto – Destroys objects.
  • Confringo – Causes explosions.
  • Incarcerous – Creates ropes to bind the target.

Healing and Manipulation Spells

  • Episkey – Heals minor wounds.
  • Ferula – Creates bandages for wounds.
  • Enervate – Revives someone who is stunned.
  • Obliviate – Erases memories.
  • Legilimens – Allows the user to read minds.
  • Confundus – Confuses the target.

Dark Spells and Unforgivable Curses

  • Avada Kedavra – Kills the victim instantly.
  • Crucio – Inflicts unbearable pain.
  • Imperio – Controls the victim’s will.
  • Sectumsempra – Cuts deeply as if with invisible swords.

Other Useful Spells

  • Expecto Patronum – Summons a Patronus to repel Dementors.
  • Muffliato – Makes nearby people hear a buzzing sound instead of conversations.
  • Finite Incantatem – Cancels the effects of other spells.
  • Gemino – Duplicates objects.
  • Reducio – Reduces the size of an object.
  • Engorgio – Enlarges the size of an object.

Movement and Object Manipulation Spells

  • Depulso – Pushes objects or people away (opposite of Accio).
  • Locomotor – Moves objects with the wand.
  • Mobilicorpus – Moves lifeless bodies by levitating them.
  • Descendo – Makes an object fall or descend.
  • Ascendio – Launches the user upward with great speed.

Advanced Defense and Protection Spells

  • Protego Totalum – Creates a large protective barrier.
  • Protego Horribilis – Protection against dark magic.
  • Protego Maxima – An advanced and powerful version of Protego.
  • Salvio Hexia – Protects an area against hostile spells.
  • Repello Muggletum – Makes Muggles avoid an enchanted area.
  • Homenum Revelio – Detects the presence of nearby people.

Transfiguration and Alteration Spells

  • Duro – Turns an object into stone.
  • Glisseo – Turns stairs into a slide.
  • Herbivicus – Accelerates the growth of plants.
  • Orchideous – Makes a bouquet of flowers appear.
  • Colovaria – Changes the color of an object.
  • Melofors – Covers the target's head with a pumpkin.
  • Evanesco – Makes objects or substances vanish.

Illusion and Mind Control Spells

  • Obscuro – Blinds the target with a magical blindfold.
  • Confundus – Confuses and disorients the victim.
  • Imperius – Forces the victim to obey (Unforgivable Curse).

Fire and Water Related Spells

  • Incendio – Creates fire.
  • Aguamenti – Produces water from the wand.
  • Glacius – Freezes objects.
  • Arresto Momentum – Slows the movement of objects or people.

Fun or Practical Spells

  • Tarantallegra – Makes the target’s legs dance uncontrollably.
  • Slugulus Eructo – Causes the victim to vomit slugs.
  • Waddiwasi – Launches small objects at high speed.

Physical Manipulation Spells

  • Alarte Ascendare – Launches an object or living being into the air.
  • Baubillious – Casts a bolt of electric light.
  • Carpe Retractum – Works like a magical hook to pull objects or people.
  • Cistem Aperio – Opens trunks and boxes forcefully.
  • Partis Temporus – Temporarily separates objects or barriers.

Dark Magic Spells

  • Morsmordre – Summons the Dark Mark of the Death Eaters.
  • Conjunctivitis – Affects the target’s vision, causing temporary blindness.
  • Defodio – Digs or opens holes in solid surfaces.
  • Oppugno – Makes objects or creatures attack a target.
  • Relashio – Releases the target from bindings, chains, or grips.

Climate and Environmental Spells

  • Ventus – Creates a strong gust of wind.
  • Meteolojinx Recanto – Stops weather effects created by magic.
  • Finestra – Breaks windows silently.
  • Periculum – Shoots red sparks into the sky (used as an emergency signal).

Communication and Writing Spells

  • Anapneo – Clears a person’s airway.
  • Specialis Revelio – Reveals hidden spells in an object.
  • Portus – Turns an object into a Portkey (teleporter).
  • Scribblifors – Transforms objects into writing quills.

Light and Darkness Related Spells

  • Umbra Scriptor – Makes shadows mimic the user’s movements.
  • Tenebris – Creates temporary darkness in an area.

Time and Space Manipulation Spells

  • Tempus – Displays the time on a clock.
  • Episkey – Helps heal minor wounds or scratches.
  • Portus – Turns an object into a Portkey, allowing teleportation.

Illusion Magic Spells

  • Holoportus – Creates an illusory image of a moving person or thing.
  • Illusio – Creates an illusion where reality is distorted, making something appear that isn’t really there.

Advanced Defense and Protection

  • Protego Horribilis – Provides stronger protection against dark spells.
  • Ferula – Creates a bandage or immobilizer for the victim’s injuries.

Environmental Protection Spells

  • Repello Muggletum – Repels Muggles from a specific area.

Miscellaneous and Curious Spells

  • Vera Verto – Turns an animal into an object (e.g., turning an animal into a cup).
